About me

Hi, I'm Laura Ngandjangola,
a web designer based in Ottumwa, IA.
I've been designing and developing websites for a while now.I have experience in HTML,CSS, and JavaScript.
I'm passionate about creating user-friendly Websites that provide an excellent user experience and
accessible websites that make a difference.

My goal is to provide my clients with websites that are not only
visually appealling but also meet their business needs. I believe that every website should be unique and reflect the client's brand and personality.

My design philosophy is to keep it simple, clean, and elegant.
I believe that less is more and that a website should be easy to navigate and understand.

When I'm not designing websites, I enjoy drawing and painting, I also enjoy reading about how the human mind works and I love to travel and explore new places.

If you have any questions or would like to discuss a project, feel free to contact me.
